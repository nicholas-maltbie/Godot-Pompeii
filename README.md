## Game Information

Game summary information from Board Game Geek - [The Downfall of Pompeii (2004)](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/13004/the-downfall-of-pompeii/files)

* Title: "The Downfall of Pompeii"
* Year Released: 2004
* Publishers:
  [AMIGO](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgamepublisher/8/amigo),
  [Maldito Games](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgamepublisher/30677/maldito-games),
  [Mayfair Games](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgamepublisher/10/mayfair-games)

Summary - Game is set in the city of Pompeii in the year 79 AD. The game is split into two parts, the first half when players fill the city with citizens. Then Vesuvius erupts and players attempt to flee the city. The player who manages to get the most players to freedom wins.
